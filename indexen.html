The fabulous parade

The Fabulous Parade is an exhibition that aims to represent the marvelous and spectacular vision of contemporary art. The exhibition will present to the spectators an opportunity to rediscover the greatest classics through the costumes of wonderful and legendary characters. Their history, from the idea to the realization through the many disappointments of costume designers. Finally, unpublished interviews with the various directors and costume designers who will explain their history with the costumes and characters as well as their various interpretations.
