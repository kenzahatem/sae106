Summary of the exhibition

The Monstrous Parade is a horrifying art exhibition featuring works by local and international artists. The exhibition explores themes of fear, horror, and the supernatural through a variety of mediums, including painting, sculpture, photography, and installation.

Summary of the interview

1. Can you tell me about your artistic organization and its objectives?
2. How do you choose the artists you exhibit?
3. What are the next exhibitions planned?
4. How do you promote your exhibitions to the public? What marketing campaign/approach did you put in place? (for example on the internet or in newspapers or other)
5. Do you have any special events or educational programs associated with your exhibits?
6. How do you finance your exhibitions / what is the cost?
7. How do you measure the success of your exhibitions?
8. Are there any plans for collaboration with other arts organizations in the future?
9. How long did it take you to set up this exhibition?
10. How did you come up with the idea of ​​creating this exhibition and why?
11. Which audience did you want to target and which audience did you most appeal to?
12. Does it work better, worse, or as well as previous exposures? Why you think ?
14. What did you want to inspire in visitors? what was the purpose?
15. How many exhibitors were there for this exhibition?
16. How did you get the show?
17. What steps did you take to get the costumes lent?
18. Are you an individual or do you work for a company?
19. Do you think it's easier to be taken seriously working in a company than working alone?
