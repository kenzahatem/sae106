Photos of the exhibition

Summary of the exhibition


The Monstrous Parade is a horrific art exhibition featuring works by local and international artists.
The exhibition explores the themes of fear, horror and the supernatural through a variety of costumes, arrangements and sets from different worlds and styles.
From science fiction to fairy tale to epic we also find sculptures, paintings, photographs as well as videos of the creators and the creative process.
We were struck by the sober but captivating atmosphere.
They managed to create a semblance of a universe for each costume with almost invisible transitions.
